Simply contact me by any means and add following info:

  • Company name
  • Website
  • City
  • Business number
  • Do you create software/hardware products?
  • Do you offer software/hardware services?
  • Company business is not IT but you have an internal development department?
  • Total employee count, male programmers count, female programmers count
  • Last year's business with government (percentage)
  • Which points of The Joel test do you fulfill?
  • List of technologies. Get inspiration here: StackOverflow tags

Put the most emphasis on writing a perfect technology profile. It should focus on listed technologies, explain how are they used. Talk about the dev environment, tools, build system, programming languages, frameworks, test coverage, team organization, etc. Classic rant during an job interview. Actually this written tech profile can be later used to attract new employees in job postings. We support Markdown format, which means that you can use headings, paragraphs, bold ... and even images too, but they have to be hosted on your server.
