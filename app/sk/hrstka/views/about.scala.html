Hŕstka was created from my need to find out where I can get a job if I want to live in Bratislava city and use Scala. I talked to friends, went to meetups and I've realized that there are many cool companies here that should be brought up to the light.

Looking for awesomeness

I'd like to bring up companies that a handful of well-chosen individuals think they are worth to work for. Hŕstka lists companies ordered by a rating based on:

  1. Used technologies
  2. Overall tech profile
  3. Business with government
  4. The Joel Test
  5. Female programmers count

Technology profile

Every tech company should have a story here. List of used technologies is nice, but a well-written novelty is priceless. Ideally this technology profile is proudly used to attract new employees.

Open data, open source, open mind

Don't be shy. Contribute ideas, critics, opinions, information about companies, create pull requests. Spread the word, share the link. It will all get back to you.
